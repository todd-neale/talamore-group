Current Development

Viney Bridge Mill

South Somerset

Nestled in the scenic surroundings of South Somerset, the Viney Bridge Mills development represents a thoughtful blend of modern living with historical charm. This project aims to revitalise the historic mill site, transforming it into a vibrant residential community.

The repurposing of Viney Bridge Mill creates the opportunity for a unique community inspired by the site's rich industrial past and surrounding local landscape and watercourse. The objective is to create a unique atmosphere blending the two characters of the site, focused around a new landscaped garden and water feature.

Whilst the development maintains a unified character, it features four distinct zones, each showcasing architectural and landscape nuances. The central pedestrian street, Mill Street, terminating in a new green space at the centre of the site, stands out as the development's defining feature.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Every development we undertake is shaped by a set of principles that drive us to build better — for residents, communities and the environment.

Quality Craftsmanship

We never compromise on build quality. Every home we create is constructed to the highest standards using premium materials and skilled tradespeople.

Sense of Place

We design developments that complement and enhance their surroundings, respecting local character and creating a genuine sense of belonging for residents and neighbours alike.

Community Focus

We are inspired by community. Every project is an opportunity to contribute positively — supporting local economies, employment, and fostering vibrant, inclusive neighbourhoods.

Integrity & Collaboration

We operate with complete transparency and honesty, working collaboratively with partners, local authorities and communities to deliver outcomes everyone can be proud of.
